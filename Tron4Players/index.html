Tron (4 Players)

Tron 4 ways:

Player 1: White Trail, use W,A,S,D to change direction.

Player 2: Red Trail, use I,J,K,L to change direction.

Player 3: Green Trail, use arrow keys to change direction.

Player 4: Blue Trail, use 8,4,5,6 (use number pad on right side of keyboard) to change direction.
